### 🌸 Cherry Blossoms in Kyoto

**April 2025 · [📍 Kyoto, Japan](https://maps.google.com/?q=Kyoto,Japan)**

<!-- PHOTO: Replace the URL below with your own image link -->
![Cherry blossoms at Maruyama Park](https://picsum.photos/seed/kyoto/800/450)
*Cherry blossoms at Maruyama Park*

We timed it just right — the sakura were in peak bloom the morning we arrived at Maruyama Park.
The trees looked like pink clouds had fallen from the sky.

We wandered through the Gion district in the late afternoon and stumbled on a tea house that had been
in the same family for 200 years. The owner spoke no English and we spoke no Japanese, but she poured
us tea with the same unhurried care she probably poured for her grandparents' guests. Absolutely unforgettable.

**Highlights:**
- Maruyama Park at dawn — arrive early before the crowds
- Fushimi Inari shrine — the thousand torii gates at sunset
- Nishiki Market for street food (the grilled mochi skewers!)

**Tags:** `Japan` `Spring` `City` `Culture`

### 🍋 Driving the Amalfi Coast

**June 2025 · [📍 Amalfi Coast, Italy](https://maps.google.com/?q=Amalfi+Coast,Italy)**

<!-- PHOTO: Replace the URL below with your own image link -->
![Cliffside village on the Amalfi Coast](https://picsum.photos/seed/amalfi/800/450)
*Cliffside village on the Amalfi Coast*

White-knuckle hairpin turns, turquoise water dropping away below the cliff edge, and the best
limoncello we've ever tasted — that's the Amalfi Coast in a nutshell.

We stayed in a tiny village called Praiano, away from the tourist crowds, and watched the sunset
paint the cliffs orange every evening from our terrace. On our last night, a local fisherman sold
us the day's catch from a cooler on the dock and we cooked it on the apartment stove with lemon
and olive oil. Simple and perfect.

**Highlights:**
- Stay in Praiano instead of Positano — same views, half the price
- Drive the coast road early morning before tour buses arrive
- Take the ferry between towns instead of driving every time

**Tags:** `Italy` `Summer` `Coast` `Food`

### 🏔️ Hiking Torres del Paine

**November 2025 · [📍 Patagonia, Argentina & Chile](https://maps.google.com/?q=Torres+del+Paine,Chile)**

<!-- PHOTO: Replace the URL below with your own image link -->
![Torres del Paine at sunrise](https://picsum.photos/seed/patagonia/800/450)
*The towers at first light*

Four days on the W trek — blisters, aching legs, and some of the most dramatic scenery on Earth.
The granite towers emerge from the clouds like something from a fantasy novel. We shared a refugio
with hikers from Brazil, Germany, and South Korea. Instant friends, the way hard trails make you.

The wind in Patagonia is something else entirely. On day two it hit us so hard on an exposed ridge
that we had to crouch and grab trekking poles just to stay upright. And then it stopped, and the
silence was absolute, and the glacier lake below us turned the color of a swimming pool.

**Highlights:**
- Book refugios 6+ months in advance — they sell out fast
- Layers, layers, layers — weather changes every 20 minutes
- Sunrise at Mirador Las Torres is worth the 2am start

**Tags:** `South America` `Hiking` `Nature` `Adventure`
