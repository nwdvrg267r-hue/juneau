## 👋 About This Blog

<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Replace with your name and bio -->

On the road again! (Admittedly I will be going the greatest distance by air, spend the most time on the ocesan, and less than 50 miles by road, but there is some mystique to being "on the road again") To me the **journey** is the best part of travel and the destination is just an excuse for the journey. In the last few years I took a cruise down the Snake and Columbia rivers, and came close to circumnavigating the continent by rail When I once again got itchy feet and starting looking for my next *journey of a lifetime*, I first looked at a rail journey down the entire length of the African continent, but was put off when I found formal dress is expected for dinner. Then there is a "small ship" cruise from the Canary islands to Morrocco still has a lot of appeal, and is on the back burner. and a tiny ship (eight passengers) around the coast of Scotland and the Hebrides to Denmark (too much togetherness for my introverted personality). 

But then I received a brochure from American Cruise Lines, the same company I had cruised the Columbia with, and was captured by the idea of cruising the inland passage from Juneau, Alaska to Tacoma, Washington with stops along the way to visit eagles, bears, seals, whale, and orca. There were no single staterooms available for almost eighteen months. That was seventeen months ago. So now my bags are packed, my passage paid and I --sail-- fly September 5. 🧳

Once again I will be sharing my journey with family and friends via the internet. So please join me on my latest odyssey.
